Frequently Asked Questions

Is this IQ test accurate?

Yes. This assessment is designed using the standards of Raven's Progressive Matrices, the most common methodology for measuring fluid intelligence and general cognitive ability (g-factor) globally.

What is a "good" IQ score?

The average score is defined as 100. Scores between 85 and 115 are considered average. A score above 130 is classified as "Gifted" (top 2% of the population).

How long does the test take?

The test is strictly timed at 20 minutes. It is designed to be challenging; do not worry if you cannot finish all questions in time.

While this test uses professional methodologies correlated with WAIS-IV, official Mensa admission requires a proctored in-person exam. This tool is for personal assessment and educational purposes.
