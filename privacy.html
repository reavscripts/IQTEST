Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how IQ Metrics Global collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this website is:
IQ METRICS GLOBAL
Contact: admin@reav.space

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Test Responses: The answers you provide to the cognitive assessment questions. These are processed anonymously to calculate your score.
  • Payment Information: We do NOT store your credit card details. All transactions are processed securely by PayPal Inc.. We only receive a confirmation of payment to unlock your result.
  • Technical Data: IP address and browser type (processed temporarily for security and site functionality).

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed solely for the following purposes:

  • To generate your IQ score and PDF certificate.
  • To process the one-time payment for the service.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of the website.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies strictly necessary for the functioning of the quiz (e.g., to remember your progress). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.

5. Data Retention

Your specific test results are processed in real-time. We do not store personally identifiable records of your IQ score on our servers after the session is closed, unless required by law for tax purposes related to the transaction.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided above.
